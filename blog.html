6/11/23

I should've posted this yesterday, but by the time I went to bed it was like 11:40 PM. So I might as well write it now. Anyway, I've (basically) 100%'d Super Mario Bros Wonder, so let me give you my thoughts on it.
Starting with the visuals - they look great. They FINALLY did stylisation in a Mario game and it looks fantastic - especially when one of the Wonder Flowers are in play and the visuals become more of a psychadellic acid trip than a video game. New Soup wishes it could be this pretty - and I like New Soup (well, the first two at least - NSMB2 and U are just kind of boring and I haven't gone around to playing NSLU because my Wii U's been broken for a few years now).
On the subject of New Soup - this game overshadows it ten-fold. There's fun mechanics (e.g. the Wonder Flower and Badges), great stages (that are also surprisingly difficult, looking at you, Badge Marathon especially), a decent selection of characters (I usually played as Mario or Blue Toad, except for some difficult levels where I played as a Yoshi - because some of the later levels are hard AF), and some other elements come together to make a pretty good game. Even the talking flowers have charm to them - despite becoming annoying if you're stuck on a segment in a level (I kept hearing "Press R in mid-air!" soooooo many times on that last level). If you like platformers, I'd recommend it. You'll need a Switch (or Yuzu), though.
-athe
P.S. By "basically 100%'d", I mean that apart from the standees, I've completed everything.
