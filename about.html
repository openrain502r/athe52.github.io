in case you don't know me, I'm a graphic designer. I'm also a huge fuckin nerd for technology, history, and a few other things as well (that's hyperfixation for you). I feel like you can identify a lot about me just via looking at the buttons above - I'm super fucking left wing (and I want solarpunk to become reality), I care about preservation of all kinds, and I like knowing stuff.

Outside tech, I also like writing and art (though I don't do either commonly... does that make me a hypocrite?), among a bunch of other stuff I'm not sharing (cause I'm not putting my life out there on the internet).

In terms of my graphic design, I usually just make operating system mockups (and have done so since around 2018 with a brief hiatus in 2023) - it's my bread and butter, despite not being 100% at it. It's to the point where I can look back at work from a few months ago and find a lot of things I want to change, but by then it's usually too late. I guess that means I nitpick a a lot.

That's about it - without delving too deep into my IRL life.
